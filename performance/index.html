Performance Notes

A win means the target was hit before the stop-loss after entry was triggered. A loss means the stop-loss was hit before the target after entry was triggered. “Pending Trigger” means the scanner idea has not yet crossed the entry trigger. “Active After Trigger” means entry was triggered, but neither stop nor target has been hit yet. “Ambiguous” means the available candle data does not reliably show whether the stop or target happened first.
